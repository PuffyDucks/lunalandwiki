$F_X(x)=P[X\leq x]$
## Properties
$F_X(-\infty)=0$  
$F_X(\infty)=1$

For all $x'\geq x$, $F_X(x')\geq F_X(x)$  
$F_X(b)-F_X(a)=P[a<X\leq b]$

For $x_i\leq x<x_{i+1}$, $F_X(x)=F_X(x_i)$
