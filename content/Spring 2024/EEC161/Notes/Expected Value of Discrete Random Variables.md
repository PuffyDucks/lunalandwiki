## Expected value

$E[X]=\mu_x=\sum\limits_{x\in S_X}xP_X(x)$  
$E[X-\mu_X]=0$  
$E[aX+b]=aE[X]+b$  
$E[X+Y]=E[X]+E[Y]$
## Expected value of [[Spring 2024/EEC161/Notes/Families of Discrete Random Variables|random variable families]]

### Geometric (p)
$E[X]=1/p$
### Binomial (n,p)
$E[X]=np$
### Pascal (n,p)
$E[X]=np$
### Discrete uniform (k,l)
$E[X]=(k+l)/2$
### Poisson (Î±)
$E[X]=\alpha$
